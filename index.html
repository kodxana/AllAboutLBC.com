LBRY Credits Information:

What is LBC Credits

"LBRY Credits (LBC) are used to publish, purchase, and support content on the LBRY network. LBC are also used to aid the discovery of content through open-source top/trending algorithms and help increase the relevancy of search results."

How to Spend Bitcoin

Bitcoin can be used to pay rent, bills, buy food, gas, almost anything public or private.

Directly - you can send Bitcoin directly to another personal or company Bitcoin Address

Indirectly - using third parties like bitrefill.com you can spend your Bitcoin online at some places that dont directly accept bitcoin

Cash Out to Fiat - using services provided by companies like CashApp cash.app you can sell your Bitcoin to USD into your bank account.

How to Store Bitcoin

Bitcoin can be stored in many ways:
Paper wallets - Bitcoins are store physically on a paper backup of the Private Key and usually also the Public Address, this can often be the safest way to store Bitcoin as long as it comes from a trusted source.


Hardware Wallets - Stores your bitcoins on Physical Devices that are computers or can be connect to computers or sometimes used as a stand alone device..
ledgerwallet.com
trezor.io
keepkey.com


Online Wallets - Often the easiest but not always the safest way to store or use bitcoin. One of the best online wallet to use if you are gonna use and online wallet is
blockchain.com
